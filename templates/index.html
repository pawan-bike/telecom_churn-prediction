Project Overview

Customer Churn Prediction is a Machine Learning project developed to help telecom companies identify customers who are likely to discontinue their services. The prediction is based on customer demographic details, subscription plans, billing information, internet services, payment methods, and contract details. This system enables businesses to take proactive measures to improve customer satisfaction and reduce customer loss.
